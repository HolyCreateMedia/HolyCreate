Get to Know Me

Hady Khalifa is an aspiring and talented professional Lebanese artist, with a great passion for tattooing. Hady has always been interested in art, he started to pursue a career in tattooing in 2015. Hady strives to create and tattoo custom pieces, which encourages a very positive experience for the clients. This allows Hady to exercise creativity and grow a vast repertoire of original art work. He enjoys working with many mediums aside from tattooing, including piercings, portraits, and graffiti. The variety of mediums practiced, enables him to develop as a well-rounded artist.

Hady has experience with many tattoo styles, such as Arabic font, portraits, abstract as well as black & gray tattoos. Though, he makes a constant effort to specialize in all tattoo styles.
